Understanding Risk Profiles

How we measure your risk tolerance

Our risk assessment helps individuals understand what is the most suitable type of Portfolio for them. There are different type of assessments based on a complex algorithm to understand the personality of the individual.

Some of the elements we assess is the ability and willingness of the individuals to take Risk but also the type of investor personality and the type of investments most suitable for them.

Preserver

A low risk taker with the desire to protect his assets.

Adventurer

Enjoy the thrill of risk, not really worries about downside but more about opportunity cost.

Visionary

Enjoy to receive in depth reports about their investments but prefer professional advisors to manage their assets.

Individualist

Prefers to manage his own assets, decided exactly how much where and wants to do his own research.
